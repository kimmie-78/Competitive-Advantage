About Us

Legal Advantage is a proud member of Competitive Advantage - Specialised Outsourcing Services.

Legal Advantage envisions labour law and industrial relations as a strategic investment for success. We are dedicated to creating a synergistic working environment that employees can call their home. We are not just a Legal service provider; we are your strategic partner when navigating the management of employees. With decades of experience in the industry and a passion for efficiency, we understand the unique needs of companies of all sizes and industries. At Legal Advantage, we aim to streamline legal processes, enhance employee motivation and engagement, and foster a positive working environment.

Lead Specialist

WILLIE ROSSOUW

Willie obtained his B Proc (cum laude) and LLB degrees at the then Randse Afrikaanse University (RAU), now the University of Johannesburg (UJ), between the years of 1990 and 1996. After a 5 year placement at the Fiji Islands he returned to South Africa focusing mainly on business law. Willie was admitted as an advocate in January 2006 and completed an Advanced Certificate in Labour Law at the University of South Africa (UNISA) in 2008. Since 2008, Willie has been involved in both business law and labour law and is currently practicing as a trust account advocate under the Legal Practice Act.
