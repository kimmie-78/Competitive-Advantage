PAYROLL ADVANTAGE

About Us

Payroll Advantage is a proud member of the Competitive Advantage - Specialised Outsourcing Services.

With a deep commitment to excellence and a team of professionals, we're dedicated to simplifying your payroll processes, allowing your company to focus on what truly matters - your growth and your employees' well-being. Our core service revolves around precise and timely payroll processing. From calculating salaries and wages to deductions and bonuses, we handle it with meticulous attention to detail. Simplify payment processes with our direct deposit services, providing convenience to your employees. Additionally, we generate detailed payslips, enhancing transparency and communication within your organisation.

Services we Offer:

Payroll Processing and Management

  • Direct deposit setup
  • Pay check generation and distribution
  • Overtime calculations
  • Bonus and commission processing
  • Accurate calculation of salaries, wages, bonuses, and deductions.
  • Timely payment processing for employees.
  • Generation of detailed payslips for transparency and communication.

Tax Services

  • Submission of EMP forms to SARS for employer declarations.
  • Handling of UIF submissions to ensure compliance with regulations.
  • Management of PAYE submissions to SARS for employee tax deductions.

Employee Benefits Administration

  • Health insurance premium deductions
  • Retirement plan contributions
  • Employee leave management (sick leave, vacation, maternity leave, etc.)
  • Workers' compensation management

Time and Attendance Solutions

  • Time clock systems
  • Online timesheets and approval workflows
  • PTO (Paid Time Off) tracking
  • Integration with payroll systems
  • Overtime and compliance monitoring

Consulting and Advisory Services

  • Payroll system setup and optimisation
  • Cost control and budgeting
  • Tax planning and optimisation

Technology Solutions

  • Cloud-based payroll software
  • Mobile payroll applications
  • Integration with accounting and ERP systems
  • User training and support
  • Troubleshooting and resolving Sage software back-office issues
